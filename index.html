Bachelor in Computer Technology

The BACT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development and network system administration. Further more, it prepare learner with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derrived fromt be BS Industri Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31 series of 2021

Program Objectives

The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multi media technology, Web'mobile development, and networks system administration, and supervisorial skills to mange an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a startup busuness or puruse a teaching career.

Specific objectives:

The learner should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develops product or processes br applying thehir knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes of products.
  4. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
  5. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunties.
